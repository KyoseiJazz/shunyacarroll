Forest Walk

August 12, 2025

Ventured deep into the old growth forest today. The sound of wind through ancient branches creates such a unique acoustic space. Recorded about 30 minutes of pure forest ambience - no human sounds, just the natural symphony.

There's something magical about how sound travels differently among these massive trees. The way echoes bounce and fade creates this incredible natural reverb that no studio can replicate.

Forest Sounds

August 11, 2025

Quick discovery session in the local woods. Found an interesting fallen log that creates a natural drum when tapped. The resonance changes depending on where you strike it.

Sometimes the best sounds come from unexpected places. Nature is the ultimate sound designer.

Summer Solstice

July 13, 2025

Spent the longest day of the year capturing the subtle changes in outdoor acoustics as the sun moved across the sky. It's fascinating how the same location can sound completely different at different times of day.

The golden hour recordings are particularly magical - there's a warmth not just in the light, but in the way sound carries during those precious moments.

Mid-Year Reflection

June 13, 2025

Halfway through the year and the sound library has grown beyond what I imagined. Each recording tells a story, captures a moment in time that can never be perfectly replicated.

Looking back at the winter recordings now, in the warmth of summer, they feel like audio postcards from another world.

Spring Project

May 13, 2025

Starting a new ambitious project to document the acoustic signature of the changing seasons. Spring is the perfect beginning - everything is waking up, and the soundscape is transforming daily.

From the first bird songs to the rustle of new leaves, each day brings something new to capture.
